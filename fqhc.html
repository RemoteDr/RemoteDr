FQHC Clinics Billing

RemoteDR supports billing for Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs), including enhanced reimbursement and specialized coding.

Common CPT Codes

  • 99499 – Unlisted evaluation and management service
  • 96160 – Health behavior assessment
  • G0466 – FQHC visit (nurse visit)
  • G0467 – FQHC visit (physician visit)

Typical Denials

  • Incorrect reporting of visit types
  • Missing documentation for grant-funded services
  • Improper bundling of procedures

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are all FQHC visits reimbursed equally?

A: No, reimbursement depends on the provider and service level.

Q: How to handle grant-funded service billing?

A: Proper documentation and coding according to payer guidelines is required.
