Pediatrics Billing

RemoteDR offers billing solutions for pediatricians, covering immunizations, well-child visits, and developmental screenings.

Common CPT Codes

  • 99381 – Initial comprehensive preventive visit
  • 90658 – Influenza vaccine administration
  • 96110 – Developmental screening
  • 99214 – Office visit, established patient (Level 4)

Typical Denials

  • Denied vaccine administration without documentation
  • Missing growth chart documentation
  • Incorrect coding of developmental screenings

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are well-child visits reimbursed separately?

A: Yes, with proper documentation of age-appropriate care.

Q: How to document vaccine administration?

A: Include vaccine name, lot number, and administration site.
