Podiatry Billing

RemoteDR offers billing services for podiatrists, including foot care, wound care, and diabetic foot management.

Common CPT Codes

  • 11721 – Debridement of nail(s)
  • 11042 – Debridement of skin and subcutaneous tissue
  • 99213 – Office visit, established patient (Level 3)
  • 28100 – Closed treatment of metatarsal fracture

Typical Denials

  • Missing documentation for wound care
  • Improper coding of surgical procedures
  • Denied diabetic foot ulcer treatments

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to document diabetic foot care?

A: Proper wound assessment and clear notes on treatment necessity are crucial.

Q: Are nail debridement procedures reimbursed separately?

A: Yes, with appropriate documentation and CPT codes.
