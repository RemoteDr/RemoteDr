Allergy & Immunology Billing

RemoteDR provides comprehensive billing support for allergists and immunologists, including allergy testing, immunotherapy, and biologics billing.

Common CPT Codes

  • 95004 – Percutaneous allergy testing (scratch test)
  • 95165 – Allergen immunotherapy preparation
  • 95115 – Single injection of allergen extract
  • 99213 – Office visit, established patient (Level 3)

Typical Denials

  • Missing documentation for allergy testing protocols
  • Lack of prior authorization for immunotherapy
  • Coding mismatch between procedure and diagnosis

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often can allergy testing be billed?

A: Most payers allow testing once per 12 months unless medically necessary.

Q: Is prior auth required for allergy shots?

A: Yes, most insurance carriers require it, especially for long-term therapy.
