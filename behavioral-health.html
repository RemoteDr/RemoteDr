Behavioral Health Billing

RemoteDR specializes in billing for behavioral health providers, including therapy, counseling, and psychiatric services.

Common CPT Codes

  • 90834 – Psychotherapy, 45 minutes
  • 90791 – Psychiatric diagnostic evaluation
  • 96127 – Brief emotional/behavioral assessment
  • 99214 – Office visit, established patient (Level 4)

Typical Denials

  • Lack of proper authorization
  • Incomplete treatment documentation
  • Incorrect use of CPT modifiers

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can telehealth visits be billed?

A: Yes, many payers now cover telehealth services for behavioral health.

Q: How to use modifiers for therapy sessions?

A: Modifier 59 or 25 may apply depending on the situation; consult payer guidelines.
