Urgent Care Billing

RemoteDR supports billing for urgent care centers, handling high patient volumes and diverse procedures.

Common CPT Codes

  • 99203 – New patient office visit (Level 3)
  • 81002 – Urinalysis, non-automated
  • 12001 – Simple repair of superficial wounds
  • 96372 – Therapeutic injection

Typical Denials

  • Improper coding of procedures
  • Lack of documentation for treatment urgency
  • Denied injection codes without proper documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to document urgent care visits?

A: Focus on presenting problem, history, and medical necessity clearly.

Q: Can injections be billed separately?

A: Yes, with proper documentation and CPT codes.
