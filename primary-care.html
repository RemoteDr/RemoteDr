Primary Care Billing

RemoteDR supports billing for primary care providers, including preventive visits, chronic disease management, and wellness exams.

Common CPT Codes

  • 99213 – Office visit, established patient (Level 3)
  • 99395 – Preventive medicine visit, 18-39 years
  • 99406 – Smoking cessation counseling
  • G0438 – Annual wellness visit, initial

Typical Denials

  • Denied preventive visits due to incorrect coding
  • Missing chronic disease documentation
  • Inconsistent use of modifiers

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to code chronic disease management?

A: Use appropriate CPT codes like 99490 with detailed clinical notes.

Q: Are wellness exams reimbursed annually?

A: Yes, per payer guidelines and patient eligibility.
