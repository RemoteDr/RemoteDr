Specialty-Specific Expertise

Every specialty has unique coding rules. Our workflows are highly calibrated for the specific nuances, codes, and typical denials of your practice.

Primary Care (PCP)

✅ Common Codes Billed

  • 99213/99214: Established Patient Visits
  • 99391-99397: Preventive Medicine Services

⚠️ Denials Mastered

  • CO-11: Diagnosis/procedure mismatch (Medical Necessity).
  • CO-97: Bundled services (Modifiers 25/59 needed).

Allergy & Immunology

✅ Common Codes Billed

  • 95004: Percutaneous Allergy Skin Tests
  • 95115/95117: Allergen Immunotherapy Injections

⚠️ Denials Mastered

  • CO-119: Benefit max reached (Exceeding max units).
  • CO-50: Non-covered (Experimental testing limits).

Behavioral Health

✅ Common Codes Billed

  • 90834/90837: Individual Psychotherapy
  • 90791: Psychiatric Diagnostic Evaluation

⚠️ Denials Mastered

  • CO-197: Missing Pre-Certification/Authorization.
  • CO-4: Missing Telehealth modifiers (95/GT).

FQHC Clinics

✅ Common Codes Billed

  • T1015: Clinic Visit / Encounter Code
  • Rev Code 0521: Rural Health Clinic/FQHC Services

⚠️ Denials Mastered

  • CO-16: Invalid info (PPS rate mismatch or missing T1015).
  • CO-22: Coordination of benefits (Medicaid crossovers).
