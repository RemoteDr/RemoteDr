OB/GYN Billing

RemoteDR provides specialized billing for obstetrics and gynecology, including prenatal care and surgical procedures.

Common CPT Codes

  • 59400 – Routine obstetric care including antepartum care
  • 58150 – Endometrial biopsy
  • 99214 – Office visit, established patient (Level 4)
  • 59000 – Amniocentesis

Typical Denials

  • Denied prenatal screenings due to missing documentation
  • Incorrect coding of surgical procedures
  • Missing authorization for high-risk pregnancy care

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to document prenatal visits?

A: Include gestational age, vital signs, and counseling notes.

Q: Are biopsies reimbursed separately?

A: Yes, with proper documentation and CPT codes.
